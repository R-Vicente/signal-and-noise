About

Understanding complexity through data

Signal & Noise is a space dedicated to applied analytics and research, focusing on understanding complex data, uncertainty, and decision-making across health, social systems, and public policy.

The goal is to find meaningful patterns - the signal - amidst the complexity and randomness - the noise - that characterizes real-world data and systems.
